About Me

I’m a Java Software Architect, based in Singapore, building Financial and Supply Chain Management systems. I’m currently developing a web application, with multi-tenant architecture, for small/medium business users to maintain their accounting data.

Work Experience:

  • ERP Application development from the very start, by designing, developing and maintaining it, together with a very talented team.
  • Created desktop application in Java with client-server communication and rich client based user interface.
  • Implemented the ERP solution in 8 different countries and supported updates to manage growing business needs / process changes.
  • Published few of my libraries as open source projects, which are listed here.

I also enjoy maintaining personal accounts and doing financial consolidation of my two entities having different functional currencies.

Personal Interests:

  • Studying Shri Satchidanandendra Saraswati Swamiji’s books from Adhyātma Prakāsha Kāryālaya.
  • Listening to Indian Classical music

Academic Qualification: Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Computer Science and Engineering (CS) from KLE Society’s College of Engineering & Technology, Belgaum affiliated to Visveswaraiah Technological University (VTU).

Blog on Accounting, Programming: Professionals and Small/Medium Businesses make lot of financial transactions which need to be accounted for reporting and statistical analysis. As Business operations continue to expand and grow, there is a greater need for system solutions to record those activities and standardization of business processes, in order to focus more on their core expertise. With the growing technology adoption in market place, providing online services to customers and access to their own data, has become a necessity.

Building such a solution for managing personal finances, also addressing requirements of Professional Service providers and Small Businesses, has been of great interest to me. This also enables me to work with latest technologies and improve my understanding of common business processes.

So my blogs will focus on topics related to accounting and associated features as well as on programming to develop such solutions.
